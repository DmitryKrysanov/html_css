Advanced Technology that is safe and effective

Santé Laser uses the FDA approved Gentle Max Pro made by Syneron - Candela, a dual wavelength laser technology that produces both 755nm alexandrite and 1064nm Nd:YAG energies. This powerful dual capability allows us to provide permanent hair reduction for all skin tones (fair, tan, and dark). The system incorporates a patented dynamic cooling device (DCD™) that protects the epidermis by applying cooling cryogen before and after every laser pulse and helps to reduce the risk of scars and pigmentation.
